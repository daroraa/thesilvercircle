Where every conversation matters

The Silver Circle connects older New Zealanders with trusted university students for genuine, paid companionship — walks, coffee, hobbies, and meaningful human connection.

Trusted by Families

  • Identity verified student companions
  • Police vetting + reference checks
  • Secure platform payments
  • Family visibility and support options
  • Human support team based in NZ
Why I Built This

This started with something personal

I saw how loneliness affects older people quietly. At the same time, students were looking for flexible and meaningful work beyond another café shift. The Silver Circle exists to solve both — creating real connection for older people and purposeful paid work for students.
